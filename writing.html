The definition of a company is to group a set of tasks that are similar and doing them all at once. Logistic companies, delivery companies, Information companies etc. I think making categories and identifying categories is the basis of intelligence. But the thing we do not know yet is how a system can identify a category organically (without looking for certain rules or telling the system to look for certain rules). Finding those patterns is the first step, then its executing a plan to make one tool to do those similar tasks or identify those similar objects.

I believe this is the basis of knowledge and intelligence (and I even want to say consciousness) And what is this fueled by? Well that's energy.
